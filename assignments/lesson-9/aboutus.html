Our Staff

Eric Mat

Eric Mat was born in San Diego, California. He began surfing at the age of 7. At the age of 13 he had gained much attention and recognition. When he is home, he enjoys spending time with his closest friend, Jaime. He has stated in many occasions that their greatest experiences have come from watching the sunset as they walk on the beach.

Linda Martin was born in Jackson, Tennessee. At the age of 4 her family migrated to Colorado Springs, Colorado. She highly enjoys hiking with her friends and family members. She has stated that she goes out for walks to relax and meditate.

John Steven
